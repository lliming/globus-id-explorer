Correcting your identity data

{{pagetitle}} obtains identity data from Globus. Globus obtains identity data from the organizations you've logged in with or have linked in Globus. These organizations are called identity providers.

When you visited {{pagetitle}}, your effective identity provider was {{primaryidp}}. Your data included information from the following linked identity providers: {{idsetproviders}}. Globus will always use the same effective identity provider for this app, but other apps may request a different effective identity provider.

Each identity provider reports your name, email address, and username. (Some also report your organization.) To correct the data reported by an identity provider, visit the identity provider's website, find your user profile, and make your changes there. The next time you authenticate using that identity provider in any app that uses Globus, the data will be updated in Globus.

Note regarding XSEDE apps: XSEDE apps request XSEDE as the effective identity provider. When you use XSEDE apps, your name, email address, username, and organization come from your XSEDE user profile.
