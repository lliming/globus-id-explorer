Correcting your identity data

{{pagetitle}} obtains identity data from Globus. Globus obtains identity data from the organizations you've logged in with or have linked in Globus. These organizations are called identity providers.

When you visited {{pagetitle}}, your effective identity provider was {{primaryidp}}. Globus will always use the same effective identity provider for this app, but other apps may request a different effective identity provider.

Your name, email address, and username come from this identity provider. (Your organization might also.) To correct this data, visit the website for the identity provider above, find your user profile, and make your changes there. The next time you authenticate using that identity provider in any app that uses Globus, the data will be updated in Globus.

Note regarding ACCESS apps: ACCESS apps request ACCESS CI as the effective identity provider. When you use ACCESS apps, your name, email address, username, and organization come from your ACCESS user profile.
